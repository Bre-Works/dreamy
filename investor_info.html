Support Dreamy


Dreamy has a wide range of user target, from children to adults. It is currently a free application. We plan to acquire money from a charged premium version of the app and some in-app purchases.

Until today, Dreamy has no real competitors in the Android market. Of course there are many other To-Do's apps, but nothing specifically for recording 'dreams', in the way we do. Compared to the good old pencil and paper, Dreamy offers more advantages than paper. It offers features that a pencil and paper cannot do; a more organised view, notifications, and many more.

What we'll do with the funds

Update the app to support more features including some in-app purchases, and release a premium version. Those are the new features that we are eager to work on:

- Cloud storage (premium): We'll save your dreams in one big cloud, so that if you change or lose your device, your dreams are never lost.
- Free time ideas (premium): Write down the things that inspire you to dream, in the format of text or images.
- Themes (in-app purchases): Currently the theme is a simple dual-color, but we're sure you'd love to personalise. New themes can be unlocked by purchase.
- Avatar (in-app purchases): Wide range of character design to accompany your widget. New avatars can be unlocked by purchase.
- Dream chest: Store your achieved dreams in a dream chest, so you don't forget how far you've come!
